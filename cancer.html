AI-Powered Breast Cancer Therapy Optimizer

Using deep learning and genomic biomarkers to personalize breast cancer treatment

About the model

Every year, millions of women face breast cancer. Choosing the right therapy is complex and depends on tumor biology, genetics, and patient history. Our Deep Q-Learning model, trained on The Cancer Genome Atlas data, simulates multiple treatment options and predicts the most effective and least toxic plan for each patient — empowering oncologists and improving outcomes.

Key results from the study:

  • Average tumor reduction: 129.71 mm
  • Minimum tumor reduction: 95.01 mm
  • Maximum tumor reduction (complete elimination): 150.0 mm
  • Side effects score below 0.0802
  • Genetic differentiation for markers TP53, KRAS, BRAF ranging from 9.53 to 12.96
